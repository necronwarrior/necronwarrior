About Me

I'm a Games Programmer living in Scotland with specialisations in tools and gameplay, examples of which you can find around this site.
I've been playing games since I could pick up a controller and thinking how they have been made since I first put it down. Exploring the editors that came with some of the games of my childhood (looking at you Warcraft 3 WorldEdit) and building copies of the games I had played in not-so-suitable programming environemts (Borland Delphi 7 Pokemon) put me on the path to where I recently acquired my Masters degree in Games Development at Abertay University.
If you've got any questions or just want to chat, feel free to contact me :)
You can find me playing : Hearthstone, Darkest Dungeon and Heroes of the Storm.
